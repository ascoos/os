# **Roadmap – Ascoos OS**

Ascoos OS is a **Web 5.0** and **WebAI** PHP core currently under development.

---

## **Goals / Philosophy**

> Ascoos OS aims to become a unified PHP Web 5.0 / WebAI core with a strong focus on automation, artificial intelligence, and natural‑language integration.

Learn how Ascoos OS implements the [Web 5.0](https://os.ascoos.com/docs/articles/ascoos-meets-web5-el.html) and [WebAI](https://github.com/ascoos/WebAI/blob/main/README.md).

---

## **Current Status**

- Current version: **1.0.0.a27**
- Stage: Pre‑Alpha / Internal builds
- Current Focus:
  - Core Kernel
  - Math & Science System
  - AI Core System
  - Macro Engine

---

## **2025**

- ✔️ Creation/upgrade of ~280 classes (AI, NLP, networking, Macro Engine, IoT).  
- ✔️ Documentation, examples, and case‑study improvements.  
- ✔️ Internal debugging builds.

---

## **2026**

- Completion of ~800 total classes.
- Expansion of advanced AI features (neural networks, NLP).
- Extended IoT support.
- Expansion of the Ascoos Macro Engine.
- ✔️ Release of **JML** (JSON‑Inspired Markup Language).
- Launch of the online **JML Studio**.
- Release of the Ascoos Documentation Builder (**DoBu**).
- Release of the **BootLib** UI Framework.
- Release of the **jAscoos** JavaScript Framework.
- Release of the **Ascoos OS Helper** VSCode extension for developers.
- Creation of the official documentation website.

---

## **2027**

- Completion of ~1500 total classes.
- Completion of companion applications.
- Completion of **WIC** (Web Image Cryptography).
- Launch of all official websites.
- Completion of **CiC** technology. [See Glossary](https://os.ascoos.com/docs/api/en/glossary.html#interpretation--integration)
- Public Commercial Beta release under **AGL** (Ascoos General License).
- First official client webpages.

---

## **2028**

- Development of 500+ native AI classes, symbolic mathematics.
- Commercial release under **AGL** (Ascoos General License).
- Completion of the **Ascoos Framework** and **Ascoos CMS Oxyzen**.

---

## **2029 – 2032**

- Completion of **LiL** (Language in Language) technology. [See Glossary](https://os.ascoos.com/docs/api/en/glossary.html#interpretation--integration).
- Full natural‑language compiler → DSL, voice commands in Greek and other languages.

---

## **2033 – 2040**

- WebAI Mesh Network - bidirectional neural‑network communication between devices.
